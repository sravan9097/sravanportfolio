### Objectives

- Improve information architecture and navigation.
- Enhance reading comfort and visual rhythm.
- Ship a performant, accessible site.

### Process

We prototyped content flows, validated with stakeholders, and codified typography and spacing with tokens.

- Reduced bounce rate and improved time-on-page.
- Clearer blog and case study navigation.
- Foundation for future AI-assisted docs.
